Emily Shapiro

Founder & CEO

Emily is a natural connector with a love for design. When couples and families across the country started reaching out for guidance on hiring designers, architects, and builders, she began making introductions and matching projects. The pattern was always the same: well-suited professionals existed, but finding them required access to hard-to-find, practical information. TALD makes access universal with curated professionals, clear education, and straightforward technology to help you hire with confidence.

Julie Potack

Head of Community

Julie is a renovation advocate with firsthand experience navigating complex projects. After transforming her own Upper West Side apartment in 2008, she went on to complete a ground-up new build, a historic suburban renovation, and the restoration of a New York City Art Deco apartment. In 2023, she founded The Renovation Concierge to fill a gap she had experienced herself: talented professionals were out there, but homeowners lacked a trusted guide to connect the dots. Now, Julie leads Community and serves as Renovation Concierge, connecting clients with aligned architects, designers, and builders while helping them move forward with clarity and confidence.
